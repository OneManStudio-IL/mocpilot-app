## 🚀 Introducing MOCPilot 2025.2.0 — Official Multi-Platform Release!

We’re excited to announce the official release of **MOCPilot 2025.2.0**, now available across **Android**, **iOS**, and **macOS**!

This update marks a major step forward for the project. The application previously known as **Controlz** has been renamed to **MOCPilot**.  
The new name better reflects the app’s purpose and future direction — serving as a unified “pilot” for your LEGO®-based creations, providing advanced control, programming capabilities, and a more intuitive experience for builders of all levels.

## ✨ What’s New in 2025.2.0?

- Unified multi-platform release for Android, iOS, and macOS  
- Improved stability, performance, and UI responsiveness  
- Updated branding and identity under the new name *MOCPilot*  
- Continued support for LEGO® Control+, BuWizz 2/3, and custom Bluetooth modules
